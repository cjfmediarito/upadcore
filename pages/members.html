Department Members

All members of UP AdCore belong to a certain department, selected when they get accepted as members, and renewed every semester. They can serve either as a manager of that department in different teams, a project head, or an AVP.

Department Projects

UP AdCore's projects are classified into departments, depending on the project's objective and contribution to the organization.

{% include creatives.html %}

HUMAN RESOURCES

The Human Resources Department (HR) is dedicated to ensuring that the people who give life to the organization are well-taken care of. They are responsible for providing the members, future members, and alumni avenues to bond and build long-lasting relationships. They make certain that all necessary updates and information concerning the members are properly disseminated, allowing open communication within the organization. Ultimately, HR aims to strengthen the commitment of the organization’s stakeholders to it.

AdDition

This team is responsible for the recruitment and pooling of quality future members that are given the chance to experience what it is like to be part of AdCore. They are also responsible for the monitoring of future members to ensure that their journey is both meaningful and memorable.

AdHere

Team AdHere serves as the bridge to the alumni. This team is dedicated to ensuring that even after graduating, alumni are still updated and informed about the activities of the organization. They also provide avenues for alumni to communicate, help, and contribute to the growth of the organization even after they graduate.

AdHesive

Overall, this team handles the life-blood of the organization – its members. They are in charge of spearheading team building and recreational activities, simple get-togethers, and providing recognition of the different members’ skills and accomplishments. This team is also mainly in charge of giving the members more benefits through its membership card and of thinking of innovative ways to make the tambayan a place for bonding and learning through interactive and value-adding activities.

PUBLIC RELATIONS AND PROMOTIONS

The Public Relations and Promotions Department (PRP) is the voice of the organization in charge of finding innovative ways to make use of promotional channels both inside and outside the university. The department ensures that the organization, its events, and its causes are communicated excellently to its target market, and that its advertisements stand out from the clutter in the industry. From strategic planning for each project to negotiating with other organizations, it’s on PRP.

University Freshie Month

The University Freshie Month (UFM) is the official welcoming celebration for freshies that aims to help them adapt more easily to life in UP. Formerly called UP Freshie Week, the UFM has expanded to cover the whole of August, filling the month up with exciting events and activities for the freshies of UP Diliman. The project is spearheaded by the University Student Council (USC) in partnership with the Office of the Vice Chancellor for Student Affairs (OVCSA).

University Job Fair

The University Job Fair is the biggest job fair in UP Diliman, organized by the Office of Counseling and Guidance (OCG), in partnership with the University Student Council (USC) and UP Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (JPIA), with UP AdCore as its sole promotional arm. The UJF team strengthens UP AdCore’s image in the university as a student-run advertising agency and one that delivers excellent output.

UP Fair

UP Fair is a week-long annual music festival with nightly themes ranging from arts to calls for social changes. It is held at the Sunken Garden and features both established and up-and-coming OPM artists. The UP Fair team is the USC’s promotions arm for the whole event.

AdVocate

AdVocate is AdCore’s public service announcement (PSA) arm which has a specific thrust and cause at its core every semester. AdVocate partners with clients (companies or organizations) and helps them forward their advocacies to a wider audience through developing creative and effective public relations campaigns.

Juiceletter

Juiceletter is the official publication and student-run advertising magazine of UP AdCore. Through informative and insightful ideas, this magazine educates and enlightens its readers on the latest trends, news, and key people in the advertising world. Juiceletter’s internal issue also serves as AdCore’s newsletter to update members about what’s happening in the organization.

SPECIAL EVENTS AND LOGISTICS

The Special Events and Logistics Department (SEaL) is the muscle of UP AdCore. The department takes charge of the logistics by systematically controlling the flow of materials and equipment. They also work on the optimization and implementation of systems, including people, materials, and information, to ensure the smooth and efficient execution of AdCore’s events and activations.

BrandHub

BrandHub is AdCore’s corporate account team, which aims to build equity for a client’s brand through brand activation events. The format of the project is tailored to fit the client’s objectives, oftentimes to generate awareness and trial. Past BrandHub projects include Unilever’s U-Tour and Pepero Day 2014.

AdCon

Advertising Conference (AdCon) is a series of talks and activities about the dynamic advertising landscape geared towards students from UP Diliman as well as other universities. Team AdCon is responsible for conceptualizing the theme of the conferences, developing the topics of the talks, tapping key speakers from the industry and inviting student delegates from different universities.

Teenspeak

Teenspeak is the organization’s biggest income generating event –an annual inter-university advertising competition in partnership with a corporate client. The competition allows the students to have a voice as consumers in tackling challenges posed by our partner brand. Teenspeak boasts of having had tie-ups with some of the Philippines’ top companies such as Coca Cola, Globe, Rexona, P&G Haircare, Oral-B and Samsung.

UNDER THE PRESIDENT

Under the President (UTP) houses projects not classified as belonging to the five main department functions. As the name suggests, these projects are administered by the President and mostly deal with UP AdCore initiatives that are not directly related with just one department function.

AdCore Reps to the VSB

The UP AdCore Representatives to the Virata School of Business (AdCore Reps to the VSB) is the official arm of AdCore responsible for uniting the organization in participating in the Business Administration Council (BAC) projects and events, making sure that AdCore remains well-visible and relevant in its home college. Projects in the first semester include Freshie and Shiftee Orientation and OrgPres (Inter-org theatrical performance presentation).




Business League NYBC AdHoc Team

The National Youth Business Convention (NYBC) is a pioneering event part of VSB’s centennial celebration. It will be a 3 to 5 day convention with top-notch speakers across industries, and is envisioned as the apex of youth business seminars in the Philippines. This special team is composed of six individuals who are AdCore’s representatives in the NYBC organizing team.

InfoCore

The Information Core (InfoCore) is the right hand of the ExeCore responsible for the collection, organization, and publication of all relevant information related to UP AdCore, both internal and external to the organization. The team is also in charge of maintaining and developing the org databases, social media accounts, and official websites.

PANA-FAO Team

The PANA-FAO Team represents UP AdCore in the Philippine Association of National Advertisers Federation of Advertising Organizations (PANA-FAO). They interact with colleagues from member schools, as well as professionals in the field of advertising and communication, through PANA. Moreover, they spearhead all PANA-FAO initiatives assigned to UP AdCore.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Your application process is as simple as spelling ADCORE. Go through with the following steps and be a part of this creativity.

A

Attend AdCore’s events. You can attend more than the required number of events. Complete the required 30 hours of Tambay. Hang out at the AdHouse and get to know other applicants and members.

D

A buddy family (composed of 2-3 members and 3-6 applicants) will have to accomplish buddy family tasks, which will have corresponding points. Each family will have to meet the required number of points announced by Team AdDition.

C

Ask the members to sign your sigsheet, especially the ExeCore and Team AdDition. Design it as creatively as you can and enjoy getting to know the members more.

O

Offer help to the AdCore teams and be an intern! This is your chance to have hands-on experience of handling an AdCore project and learning how teams work in AdCore.

R

In advertising, the pitching of ideas is what we call a Revalida. This incorporates all the learnings you and your groupmates acquired throughout your application as you present to the ExeCore and Team AdDition.

E

This will test your knowledge about AdCore and your dedication to join the organization.


UP Advertising Core holds its application process every semester. If you are a UP Diliman student looking for an avenue to express your creativity, this might just be the organization for you! Keep posted on our Facebook page to learn more about how to apply to UP AdCore.
