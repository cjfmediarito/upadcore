UP Advertising Core

The UP Advertising Core is the only student-run, not-for-profit organization and advertising agency of the University of the Philippines, Diliman.

It is based in the Virata School of Business and is a university-wide organization with members coming from various disciplines, mainly from Business Administration and Marketing, Mass Communications, and Fine Arts.
