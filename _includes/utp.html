Under the President

The Under the President (UTP) Department is a pseudo-department that houses projects that are related to more than one department function.

InfoCore

InfoCore is AdCore's one-stop information hub and branding arm. The team is tasked with the collection, organization, and dissemination of all information relevant to AdCore, as well as the handling of AdCore’s social media accounts.

COMELEC

COMELEC handles the electoral process for the next year’s new slate of ExeCore. It conceptualizes and executes each stage of the said process, from the promotional campaign down to the release of the winners. It also ensures a clean election and maintains the integrity of each member’s vote.
